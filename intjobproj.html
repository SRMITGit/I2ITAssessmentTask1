Projects and Ideas

Li-Fi Systems For Cafes

Overview

This system will be a customisable lighting system for cafes to deliver their Internet access over Li-Fi (light Wi-Fi) to their customers. The system has the potential to deliver uninterrupted, high speed, secure Internet access whilst the customers enjoy a meal or beverage. The customisable lighting system will allow café owners to outfit their spaces using their original lighting layout or additional lamps for specific areas. The cafe owners will have the ability to control access to their Internet connection simply by turning off a light or by using a dedicated button to only turn off the Li-Fi capabilities of the light.

Motivation

Café culture plays an important role in today’s always-online-society and with the ever increasing demand to be constantly connected, the safety of this connection should be something that is built in to every device. When sitting at a café enjoying your best life, using the free Internet access, you shouldn’t have to worry about some nefarious character intercepting your communication. Global cybersecurity company Kaspersky suggests the biggest threat to free Wi-Fi security is the ability for the hacker to position themselves between you and the connection point. Allowing the hacker to access to every piece of information you're sending out on the Internet[1]. Free public Wi-Fi networks usually have poor security features but by using this customisable Li-Fi system, better security measures like geofencing[3] can be automatically added for each connection quickly and easily and once you leave the light, your access will be conveniently switched off.
